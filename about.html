The Designer

A vision born from
passion for fabric

Rich Sabinian is a renowned Kapampangan fashion designer based in Pampanga, Philippines, known for creating intricate gowns and tailored suits. As the CEO of RS Gowns and Suits Shop, he has designed for beauty queens, special events, and local celebrities.

His journey began far from the spotlight — as a factory worker. Through relentless hard work and consistency, he transitioned into professional fashion design in the early 2010s.

Behind his success is a story defined by the true realities of entrepreneurship — puyat, pagod, at sakripisyo.
